About Us

Relier in french means connect. Relier the web application created by Mudit Tyagi and Naman Mathur of SRM University, Haryana as a final year project in the hope to connect the homeless and poor people with the society through donations. Making donations and helping others to grow gives a sense of happiness and fulfilment. This application is just a step forward to create connected and happy world. It is a new way for people to donate the goods for the needy people and track their donations and using the blockchain technology to store the donations data gives a better security and reliability to the web application. This web application is a perfect example of how we can use blockchain technology in various sectors of our life.
